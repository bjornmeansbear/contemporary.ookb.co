Speaker Series.

Visiting artists play an important role in public understanding and education of contemporary art. Our annual Speaker Series brings internationally-recognized artists, critics, and art professionals to Baltimore to share their practices and inspire, awe, and challenge our home audience. In the spirit of furthering contemporary art dialogue, each speaker also conducts studio visits with local artists.
