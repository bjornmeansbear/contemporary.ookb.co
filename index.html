The Contemporary

The Contemporary is coming back! After suspending operations in May 2012 the Board of Trustees has spent the past year reimagining the organization's mission, vision and operating structure.

We are currently taking the necessary actions to create a sustainable model that is a viable asset to Baltimore. We are now The Contemporary, shedding the word “Museum” from our title and redefining the capacity in which we are able to present the most innovative art of our time. We are engaging community members and stakeholders to best understand what we can do to contribute to the cultural landscape of Baltimore.

We're excited to be back and will be making a more formal announcement over the coming months. If you have any questions, comments or concerns please feel free to email us at info@contemporary.org

Or, better yet, stay connected with us on Facebook, Twitter or Instagram, or by signing up for our mailing list
