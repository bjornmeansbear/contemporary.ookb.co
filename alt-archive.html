Upcoming Projects.

The Contemporary believes that art belongs in all aspects of society because it is a catalyst for creative thinking and innovation. We are committed to commissioning site-specific and subject-oriented projects that exist in public space and are in collaboration with other existing, arts and non-arts institutions and organizations.

We are, at our core, a presenting institution that is invested in working with artists to incubate innovative projects and re-contextualize existing works that speak to many diverse audiences. We believe that contemporary art embodies a tremendous range of identities and we will continue our tradition of working with local, regional, national, and international artists at all stages of their careers. In keeping with our desire to dissolve boundaries, we will expand our programming by reaching out to creative minds in non-arts fields; architects, engineers, philosophers and more will play an important part in our project development and public programming.

We’re forever committed to remaining nimble and forward thinking. We will be announcing our upcoming projects soon.
